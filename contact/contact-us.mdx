## Office Hours

The TARMAC team hosts weekly office hours every Thursday from 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM IST. Drop in to:

- Get live help with implementation questions
- Review your designs with the team
- Discuss upcoming features and roadmap
- Share feedback and suggestions

<Info>
  Office hours are open to all designers and developers using TARMAC. No appointment needed.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I install TARMAC in my project?">
    Follow our [installation guide](/getting-started/installation) for step-by-step instructions on adding TARMAC to your React project via npm.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I customize TARMAC components?">
    Yes. TARMAC components support theming through design tokens. You can override token values to match your brand while maintaining accessibility standards.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which browsers does TARMAC support?">
    TARMAC supports the latest two versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Mobile support includes iOS Safari and Android Chrome.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I report an accessibility issue?">
    Open a GitHub issue with the "accessibility" label. Include the component name, expected behavior, actual behavior, and the assistive technology you're using.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is TARMAC open source?">
    TARMAC is currently an internal design system for Delhivery. Contact the team for access and licensing details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
