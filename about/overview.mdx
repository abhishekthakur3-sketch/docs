## Introduction TARMAC

TARMAC is more than a component library. It's a complete design system that empowers teams to build exceptional user experiences with consistency, speed, and accessibility at its core.

<Note>
**TARMAC** stands for a solid foundation upon which great products are built. Like the material that provides a smooth, reliable surface for movement, our design system creates a seamless path from design to development.
</Note>

## What is TARMAC?

TARMAC serves as the single source of truth for design and development decisions across your organization. It provides:
